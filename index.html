Our Story

Two worlds.
One business.

Vyom was founded on a simple observation: the UK and European market for India's finest natural, herbal and Ayurvedic products is significantly underserved — not because the demand isn't there, but because the right bridge hasn't existed.

Our founder grew up in India and has lived and worked in the UK for over a decade, building careers in both countries. That dual perspective — knowing instinctively how Indian producers operate and what UK buyers actually need — is the foundation of everything we do.

We are not a marketplace. We are not a broker. We are a committed distribution partner — managing compliance, building direct producer relationships, and standing behind every product we bring to market.
